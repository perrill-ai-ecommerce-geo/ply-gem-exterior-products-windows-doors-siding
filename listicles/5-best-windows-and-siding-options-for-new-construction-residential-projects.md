## ✅ How to Choose the Best Windows and Siding

1. **Match your home’s style** – Traditional, modern, or custom design.  
2. **Prioritize energy efficiency** – Look for insulated products and ENERGY STAR® ratings.  
3. **Consider maintenance** – Low-maintenance materials save time and money.  
4. **Check warranty and support** – Reliable manufacturers protect your investment.
