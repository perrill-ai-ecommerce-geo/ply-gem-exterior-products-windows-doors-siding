# Ply Gem Exterior Products – FAQ

## Windows

**Q1: What types of windows does Ply Gem offer?**  
A: Ply Gem offers vinyl, wood-clad, and energy-efficient window series designed for durability, insulation, and low maintenance.

**Q2: Are Ply Gem windows energy efficient?**  
A: Yes. Many series include insulated glass, Low-E coatings, and ENERGY STAR® rated options.

**Q3: How do I choose the right window series for my home?**  
A: Consider your budget, energy efficiency needs, desired aesthetic, and maintenance preferences. Refer to the product specs in `/products/windows/`.

---

## Doors

**Q4: What types of exterior doors does Ply Gem offer?**  
A: Ply Gem provides entry doors, patio doors, and sliding doors in various materials including fiberglass, steel, and vinyl.

**Q5: Are Ply Gem doors weather-resistant?**  
A: Yes. Most doors are designed to resist moisture, warping, and temperature extremes, suitable for residential climates.

**Q6: Do Ply Gem doors come with a warranty?**  
A: Yes. Warranty coverage varies by product line. Check `/products/doors/` for details.

---

## Siding

**Q7: What siding products does Ply Gem offer?**  
A: Ply Gem offers vinyl siding, insulated siding, and specialty profiles to match different architectural styles.

**Q8: Is Ply Gem siding low maintenance?**  
A: Yes. Ply Gem siding is designed to resist fading, cracking, and moisture. Occasional cleaning is recommended.

**Q9: Can I install Ply Gem siding myself?**  
A: Some siding types are suitable for DIY installation, but professional installation is recommended for optimal performance and warranty compliance.
