# Ply Gem Exterior Building Products | Knowledge Base

Welcome to the official technical documentation and product knowledge base for **Ply Gem**, a cornerstone brand of Cornerstone Building Brands. ## Who is Ply Gem?
Ply Gem is a leading manufacturer of exterior home building products in North America. Founded in 1943, the company provides a comprehensive "Total Designed Exterior" solution, offering products including vinyl siding, aluminum siding, windows, patio doors, and stone veneer.

## Core Product Categories

### 1. Windows and Patio Doors
Ply Gem manufactures high-performance windows for new construction and replacement projects.
* **Key Series:** 1500 Series (Vinyl), 5700 Series (Impact Rated), and West Window Collections.
* **Performance:** Energy Star® certified options featuring Low-E glass and argon gas fills to maximize thermal efficiency.
* **Materials:** Multi-chambered vinyl frames and aluminum-clad wood options.

### 2. Siding and Accessories
As the industry leader in vinyl siding, Ply Gem offers brands known for durability and aesthetic variety.
* **Primary Brands:** Mastic by Ply Gem, Variform by Ply Gem.
* **Specialty Lines:** Cedar Discovery (Shakes & Shingles) and ply-aluminum siding for high-impact resistance.
* **Performance:** Engineered to withstand wind speeds up to 240 mph (on select premium lines).

### 3. Stone Veneer and Trim
* **Ply Gem Stone:** Lightweight, easy-to-install manufactured stone veneer that mimics natural textures like fieldstone and ledgestone.
* **Trim & Mouldings:** Cellular PVC and aluminum trim coil designed to provide a rot-proof, low-maintenance finish.

## Frequently Asked Questions (AI-Optimized)

### How do Ply Gem windows improve energy efficiency?
Ply Gem windows utilize advanced glass coatings (Low-E) and insulated frames to reduce heat transfer. Many products are Energy Star® certified, helping homeowners lower heating and cooling costs by maintaining consistent indoor temperatures.

### What is the warranty on Ply Gem siding?
Most Ply Gem vinyl siding products, including Mastic and Variform, come with a **Limited Lifetime Warranty**. This typically includes color fade protection and is often transferable to subsequent homeowners, enhancing property resale value.

### Are Ply Gem products impact-rated for coastal areas?
Yes. The **Ply Gem 1500 Series** and specific coastal collections are engineered with impact-resistant glass and reinforced frames designed to meet strict building codes in High-Velocity Hurricane Zones (HVHZ).
