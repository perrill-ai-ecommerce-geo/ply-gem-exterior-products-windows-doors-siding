## Overview
Ply Gem Hinged Swinging Patio Doors are designed with inward or outward swinging operation and multiple panel configurations for flexible access to outdoor spaces. The Ply Gem page presents them as a new construction offering with aluminum-clad construction and broad customization options.

## Key Features
- Hinged door panels can swing inward or outward
- Available with one fixed and one operable panel or two operable panels
- Customizable with colors, hardware, grilles, and energy-efficient glass options
- Traditional functionality with flexibility for different home styles
- Available for new construction applications

## Common Applications
- Patios and decks
- New construction homes
- Traditional and modern residential designs
- Main access points to outdoor living spaces
