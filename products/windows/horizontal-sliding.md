## Overview
Ply Gem Horizontal Sliding Windows are designed for easy side-to-side operation and are often used in wider wall openings. This style supports simple functionality, broad outdoor views, and compatibility with multiple Ply Gem product lines.

## Key Features
- Horizontal sliding operation
- Good fit for wide openings
- Available across multiple Ply Gem series
- Offered in several material categories depending on series
- Suitable for new construction and replacement needs

## Common Applications
- Contemporary homes
- Wide wall openings
- Bedrooms and family rooms
- Replacement window projects
