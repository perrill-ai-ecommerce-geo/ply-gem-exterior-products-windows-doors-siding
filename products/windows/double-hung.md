## Overview
Ply Gem Double Hung Windows are a versatile window style with two operable sashes that support flexible ventilation and a familiar residential appearance. They are offered across multiple Ply Gem series and fit a wide range of home styles and project needs.

## Key Features
- Classic double hung configuration
- Two operable sashes for flexible airflow
- Available in multiple Ply Gem series
- Offered in several material categories depending on product line
- Suitable for new construction and replacement projects

## Common Applications
- Whole-home window packages
- Bedrooms and living spaces
- Renovation and replacement work
- Traditional and transitional homes
