## Overview
Ply Gem Picture Windows are fixed windows designed to maximize daylight and views. They are often used as focal-point windows or combined with operable units and are available across multiple Ply Gem product series.

## Key Features
- Fixed picture window style
- Designed for expanded glass area and views
- Often paired with operable windows
- Available across multiple Ply Gem series
- Suitable for new construction and replacement projects

## Common Applications
- Living rooms
- Great rooms
- View-oriented spaces
- Combination window assemblies
