## Overview
Ply Gem Casement Windows are hinged windows that open outward, making them a strong fit for homeowners seeking ventilation, clean sightlines, and a more contemporary window style. They are available across multiple Ply Gem series and project types.

## Key Features
- Outward-opening casement style
- Designed for ventilation and view enhancement
- Available in multiple Ply Gem series
- Offered in several material categories depending on series
- Suitable for new construction and replacement applications

## Common Applications
- Kitchens
- Living areas
- Contemporary home designs
- New construction and remodeling projects
