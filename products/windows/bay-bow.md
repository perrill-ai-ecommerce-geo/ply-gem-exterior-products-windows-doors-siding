## Overview
Ply Gem Bay Windows and Bow Windows are projection-style window configurations that extend outward from the home to create dimension, increase glass area, and enhance curb appeal. They can be used to add design impact as well as a broader field of view.

## Key Features
- Outward-projecting bay or bow configuration
- Creates architectural dimension
- Expands views and daylight potential
- Available through Ply Gem window offerings
- Suitable for feature areas in new construction and remodeling

## Common Applications
- Front elevations
- Living rooms
- Dining areas
- Reading nooks
- Feature window walls
