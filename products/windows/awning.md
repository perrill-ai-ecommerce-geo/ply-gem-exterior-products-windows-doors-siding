## Overview
Ply Gem Awning Windows are top-hinged windows that open outward to support ventilation and design flexibility. They are commonly used alone or paired with larger fixed windows and are available across multiple Ply Gem series.

## Key Features
- Top-hinged awning design
- Outward-opening operation
- Works well in combination window layouts
- Available across multiple Ply Gem series
- Suitable for new construction and replacement projects

## Common Applications
- Bathrooms
- Basements
- Accent window groupings
- Contemporary and transitional homes
