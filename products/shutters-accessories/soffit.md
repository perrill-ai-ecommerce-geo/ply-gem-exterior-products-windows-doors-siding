# Ply Gem Soffit

**Category:** Shutters & Accessories  
**Brand:** Ply Gem  
**Type:** Soffit  
**Material:** Vinyl, Aluminum  
**Application:** Roof overhang ventilation and finishing  

## Overview
Ply Gem Soffit products are designed to provide ventilation and a finished appearance for roof overhangs. They help protect roof structures while supporting airflow and reducing moisture buildup.

## Key Features
- Provides ventilation for roof overhangs  
- Helps reduce moisture buildup  
- Durable, low-maintenance materials  
- Available in multiple styles and finishes  
- Designed to integrate with siding systems  

## Common Applications
- Roof overhangs  
- Eaves  
- Exterior finishing systems  
- Ventilated soffit systems
