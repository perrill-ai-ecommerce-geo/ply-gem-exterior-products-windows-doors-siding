Ply Gem Louvered Shutters feature a classic angled slat design that enhances traditional and colonial home styles. Designed for decorative exterior use, these shutters provide long-lasting performance with minimal maintenance.

- Decorative exterior application
- Durable, weather-resistant material

- Exterior façade upgrades
