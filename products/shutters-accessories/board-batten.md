# Ply Gem Board & Batten Shutters

**Category:** Shutters & Accessories  
**Brand:** Ply Gem  
**Type:** Board & Batten Shutters  
**Material:** Polypropylene / Vinyl

## Overview
Ply Gem Board & Batten Shutters provide a rustic, vertical panel design that enhances farmhouse, cottage, and traditional home styles. These shutters are designed for decorative exterior use and offer low-maintenance performance with durable polymer construction.

## Key Features
- Vertical board-and-batten style
- Designed for decorative exterior applications
- Durable, low-maintenance material
- Available in multiple colors and sizes
- Resistant to moisture, insects, and weathering
- Lightweight and easy to install

## Common Applications
- Farmhouse-style homes  
- Cottage and rustic exteriors  
- Window accents  
- Decorative façade enhancements
