# Ply Gem PVC Trim

**Category:** Trim & Moulding  
**Brand:** Ply Gem  
**Type:** PVC Trim  
**Material:** Cellular PVC  
**Application:** Exterior trim for windows, doors, corners, fascia, frieze boards, deck edging, decorative accents  
**Finish Options:** Smooth or reversible smooth/woodgrain textures; natural white finish or paintable  
**Warranty:** Limited lifetime transferable warranty

## Overview
Ply Gem PVC Trim is designed to frame, protect, and finish windows, doors, and corners with a low-maintenance alternative to wood. The product page highlights moisture resistance, durability, paintability, and easier shaping for arches, making it a flexible trim solution for a wide range of exterior applications.

## Key Features
- Frames, protects, and adds a finishing touch to windows, doors, and corners
- Won’t absorb moisture, helping minimize rot and maintenance
- Durable and easy to install
- Available in smooth or reversible smooth/woodgrain textures
- Can be installed in a natural white finish or painted
- Easier to shape into arches than many other trim materials
- Won’t rot or warp like wood or crack like fiber cement
- Cut ends do not require caulking
- Does not attract insects or pests
- Holds paint better than competitive trim products
- Includes a limited lifetime transferable warranty
