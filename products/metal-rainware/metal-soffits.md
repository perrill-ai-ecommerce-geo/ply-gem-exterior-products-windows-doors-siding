# Ply Gem Metal Soffits

**Category:** Metal Rainware  
**Brand:** Ply Gem  
**Type:** Metal Soffits  
**Material:** Aluminum  
**Application:** Roof overhang ventilation and finishing  

## Overview
Ply Gem Metal Soffits are designed to provide ventilation and a finished look for roof overhangs while helping protect structural components from moisture buildup.

## Key Features
- Supports airflow and ventilation  
- Durable aluminum construction  
- Helps reduce moisture buildup  
- Low-maintenance performance  
- Integrates with fascia and gutter systems  

## Common Applications
- Roof overhangs  
- Eaves  
- Ventilated soffit systems
