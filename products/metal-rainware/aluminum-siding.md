# Ply Gem Aluminum Siding

**Category:** Metal Rainware

## Overview
Ply Gem Aluminum Siding is designed as a durable exterior cladding solution that provides protection against weather while maintaining a clean, finished appearance. It is positioned as a low-maintenance alternative to traditional siding materials.

## Key Features
- Durable aluminum construction  
- Designed to resist weather exposure  
- Low-maintenance exterior solution

- Available in multiple colors and profiles

- Residential exterior siding  
- Replacement siding projects  
- New construction homes
