# Ply Gem Cascade Ledge

**Category:** Stone Veneer  
**Brand:** Ply Gem  
**Type:** Mortared Stone Veneer  
**Profile:** Cascade Ledge  
**Application:** Interior, Exterior  
**Installation Style:** Drystack  
**Warranty:** 50 Year Warranty

## Overview
Ply Gem Cascade Ledge is a mortared stone veneer profile designed for contemporary and transitional designs. It uses a tight-fitting mix of ledge stones and cobble jumpers to create long, clean lines and a detailed textured face.

## Key Features
- Ledge-style profile for modern exteriors and accents
- Mix of ledge stones and cobble jumpers
- Clean horizontal lines
- Detailed face texture
- Suitable for interior or exterior installation
- Matching corners available
- 50 Year Warranty

## Available Colors
- Summit
- Sandy Creek
- Mystic
- Alpine Mist

## Product Details
- Individual stones average thickness: 1 ½ inches
- Individual stones length (vertical height): 1 ¼ – 6 inches
- Individual stone width: 5 ½ – 18 ¼ inches
- Drystack application cartons yield 8.5 sq ft flats
- Flats: 10 sq ft coverage per carton
- Flats carton weight: 100 lbs
- Corners: 5 linear ft coverage per carton
- Corners carton weight: 75 lbs
- 16 cartons per pallet

## Common Applications
- Contemporary home exteriors
- Columns
- Entry accents
- Fireplace surrounds
- Interior statement walls
