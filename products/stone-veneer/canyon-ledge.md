# Ply Gem Canyon Ledge

**Category:** Stone Veneer  
**Brand:** Ply Gem  
**Type:** Mortared Stone Veneer  
**Profile:** Canyon Ledge  
**Application:** Interior, Exterior  
**Installation Style:** Drystack  
**Warranty:** 50 Year Warranty

## Overview
Ply Gem Canyon Ledge is a mortared stone veneer profile designed for clean, linear installations with a contemporary feel. It combines ledge stones and cobble jumpers with layered texture and color to create a natural canyon-inspired appearance.

## Key Features
- Contemporary ledge-style profile
- Mix of ledge stones and cobble jumpers
- Detailed face texture with long, clean lines
- Suitable for interior or exterior installation
- Matching corners available
- 50 Year Warranty

## Available Colors
- Hampton
- Winfield
- Midnight Slate
- Mystic
- Summit
- Niagara
- Chardonnay

## Product Details
- Individual stones average thickness: 1 ½ inches
- Individual stones length (vertical height): 1 ¼ – 6 inches
- Individual stone width: 5 ½ – 18 ¼ inches
- Drystack application cartons yield 8.5 sq ft flats
- Flats: 10 sq ft coverage per carton
- Flats carton weight: 100 lbs
- Corners: 5 linear ft coverage per carton
- Corners carton weight: 75 lbs
- 16 cartons per pallet

## Common Applications
- Contemporary exteriors
- Accent walls
- Fireplaces
- Entryways
- Interior feature walls
