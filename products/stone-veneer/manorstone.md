# Ply Gem Manorstone

**Category:** Stone Veneer  
**Brand:** Ply Gem  
**Type:** Mortared Stone Veneer  
**Profile:** Manorstone  
**Application:** Interior, Exterior  
**Installation Style:** Mortared / Grouted  
**Warranty:** 50 Year Warranty

## Overview
Ply Gem Manorstone is a mortared stone veneer profile that blends field and ledge stones with a smooth, split face. Its varied shapes and sizes create a natural, organic look that works across interior and exterior settings as well as modern and traditional designs.

## Key Features
- Blend of field and ledge stones
- Smooth, split face
- Varied shapes and sizes
- Natural, organic appearance
- Suitable for interior or exterior installation
- Matching corners available
- 50 Year Warranty

## Available Colors
- Summit
- Oswego

## Product Details
- Individual stone average thickness: 1 ½ inches
- Individual stone length (vertical height): 1 ¼ – 10 ½ inches
- Individual stone width: 5 ¼ – 20 inches
- Suitable for interior or exterior installation
- Matching corners available
- Flats: 10 sq ft coverage per carton
- Flats carton weight: 100 lbs
- Corners: 5 linear ft coverage per carton
- Corners carton weight: 75 lbs
- 16 cartons per pallet

## Common Applications
- Mixed-style residential exteriors
- Interior accent walls
- Fireplace surrounds
- Entry features
- Modern and traditional homes
