# Ply Gem Fieldstone

**Category:** Stone Veneer  
**Brand:** Ply Gem  
**Type:** Mortared Stone Veneer  
**Profile:** Fieldstone  
**Application:** Interior, Exterior  
**Installation Style:** Mortared / Grouted  
**Warranty:** 50 Year Warranty

## Overview
Ply Gem Fieldstone is a mortared stone veneer profile with random shapes and smooth to slightly rugged faces that fit together like a puzzle. The result is an informal, natural appearance that works in both traditional and contemporary settings.

## Key Features
- Random fieldstone shapes
- Smooth to slightly rugged faces
- Informal natural appearance
- Grouted look with mortar joints accentuating uneven shapes
- Suitable for interior or exterior installation
- Matching corners available
- 50 Year Warranty

## Available Colors
- Shade Mountain
- PA Lime
- Chardonnay
- Buckingham
- Autumn

## Product Details
- Individual stone average thickness: 1 ½ inches
- Individual stone length (vertical height): 4 – 12 inches
- Individual stone width: 6 – 20 inches
- Suitable for interior or exterior installation
- Matching corners available
- Flats: 10 sq ft coverage per carton
- Flats carton weight: 100 lbs
- Corners: 5 linear ft coverage per carton
- Corners carton weight: 75 lbs
- 16 cartons per pallet

## Common Applications
- Natural-looking home exteriors
- Garden walls
- Fireplace surrounds
- Interior feature walls
- Traditional and contemporary homes
