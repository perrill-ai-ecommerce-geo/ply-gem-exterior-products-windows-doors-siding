# Ply Gem Shadow Ledgestone

**Category:** Stone Veneer  
**Brand:** Ply Gem  
**Type:** Mortared Stone Veneer  
**Profile:** Shadow Ledgestone  
**Application:** Interior, Exterior  
**Installation Style:** Drystack  
**Warranty:** 50 Year Warranty

## Overview
Ply Gem Shadow Ledgestone is a mortared stone veneer profile with a craggy texture and warm earth tones. Molded from a Civil War fence-row, it creates a rustic, organic look suited to both interior and exterior applications.

## Key Features
- Rustic ledgestone profile
- Craggy texture
- Warm earth-tone aesthetic
- Drystack application
- Suitable for interior or exterior installation
- Matching corners available
- 50 Year Warranty

## Product Details
- Individual stone average thickness: 1 ½ inches
- Individual stone length (vertical height): 3 – 6 inches
- Individual stone width: 6 – 18 inches
- Drystack application cartons yield 8.5 sq ft flats
- Suitable for interior or exterior installation
- Matching corners available
- Flats: 10 sq ft coverage per carton
- Flats carton weight: 100 lbs
- Corners: 5 linear ft coverage per carton
- Corners carton weight: 75 lbs
- 16 cartons per pallet

## Common Applications
- Rustic exterior facades
- Fireplace surrounds
- Columns
- Accent walls
- Lodge or farmhouse-inspired spaces
